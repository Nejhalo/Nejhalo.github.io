My story.

I was born in 1999 and as a child, I was very curious and loved exploring everything around me. I would spend hours creating new things and coming up with innovative ideas to make my toys even better. I was always passionate about learning and would often ask questions to understand how things worked. My parents encouraged my curious nature and provided me with the necessary resources to help me grow. Looking back, I am grateful for my childhood experiences as they have helped shape me into the person I am today.


As I grew up, I discovered that I am someone who seeks to learn, grow, and connect with people who dream big and believe they can resolve problems and build solutions to affect the global community positively. I had a passion for computer science at a young age and during my studies, I discovered a specific interest in the relationship between users and computers. This led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Software Computer. I am happy to inform you that I have successfully passed the entrance exam and ranked 37th for an associate's degree and 194th for a Software engineering bachelor's degree.


During my graduate studies, I was offered a chance to work with a small team. This team pushed me to new heights, drilling down a foundation of learning, curiosity, and honesty. Through all my adventures, what I learned is that I knew very little. After University, I took some courses in this field and worked as a freelancer which taught me that technology cannot make a difference. People can. Technology can enable it, but people must take action to shape the narrative.


My expertise lies in creating intelligent systems that learn from data and simplify technology. I specialize in designing digital products with a focus on meeting their needs. I'm now seeking a Ph.D. degree not to specialize in one thing, but to challenge myself and better understand the intricate ways in which technology and people interact. My ultimate aim is to contribute my skills to teams that build systems to solve various problems for people all around the world. These small solutions will come together, forming a bridge between nations.


I believe that attending university or a job and interacting, listening, soaking in knowledge, and contributing to a talented pool of individuals will help provide the necessary skills required to propel my career to tackle issues on a global level. I'm confident that my research experience and academic achievements qualify me as a strong candidate for this program. I'm excited about the prospect of contributing to the research.
