Case Study: Aria Health App

This is a censept project.

Overview

Aria Health is a mobile application designed to provide users with a holistic health management experience. The app helps users track their medical history, manage medications, monitor physical activity, and access health education resources. With a focus on ease of use and seamless integration with healthcare professionals, the app is a one-stop solution for personal health management.

Research

Conducted user interviews with 10 individuals to understand their health management needs and pain points. The key insights were:

  • Users struggled with managing and recalling their medication schedules.
  • Many users found it difficult to consolidate health information from different sources.
  • People wanted a feature to share health data with their doctors easily.
  • Users preferred automated reminders for health tasks, like medication and appointments.

    • Design Process

    1. Wireframes & User Flow

  • Profile Creation: Users input personal information, medical history, and medication details.
  • Health Tracking: Integrated daily logs for tracking physical activity, water intake, and nutrition.
  • Medical Records: Users can upload and view their medical history and test results.
  • Doctor Connection: Direct integration for users to share their health data with their healthcare providers.
  • Reminders: Automated reminders for medications, appointments, and vaccinations.

  • Developed mid-fidelity wireframes based on the user flow. Each screen was designed to:
  • Be minimalistic yet informative.

  • Feature clear and simple navigation to guide users to the next action, whether it's updating records, setting a reminder, or contacting a doctor.
  • Created a dashboard with an overview of daily health metrics like steps, hydration, and medication schedule (see image below for a detailed preview).

    • 3. Visual Design

  • The design language was inspired by a calm and trustworthy color palette, using soft blues and greens to evoke a sense of health and wellness.
  • Typography was chosen for clarity and readability, ensuring that users of all ages could interact with the app effortlessly.

    • 4. Usability Testing

  • I conducted two rounds of usability testing with 5 users in each round. Feedback from the first round included:
  • Simplifying the navigation from the health dashboard to detailed records.
  • Adding push notifications for missed medications.
  • After implementing these changes, the second round of testing showed significant improvements in user satisfaction and task completion.

    • Outcome


    The final design improved users' ability to manage their health and medication, making them feel more in control of their well-being. Key outcomes included:

  • 85% user satisfaction during testing.
  • Increased user engagement with daily reminders and health logs.
  • Positive feedback from healthcare professionals on the app’s ability to simplify patient data access.
